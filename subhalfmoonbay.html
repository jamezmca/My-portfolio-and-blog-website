Halfmoon Bay Marina

Breakwater Design

Design engineer for the design of an innovative breakwater system facilitating a large-scale expansion of the marina. This involves first understanding the wave dynamics and dynamic response of the structure, followed by complex system modelling allowing the specification of products for construction.

The client required the design of a new Breakwater system to ensure the safety and damage prevention of all boats harboured within the marina in the event of high risk tidal event. I was involved in the design of the structural wave absorption system that would shield the boats in the marina from any significant tidal action.

This project required regular communication with the client, constantly updating our design to their ever changing design requirements.
