GoldenEars Forcemain

Tunnelling Analysis

On this project, I was the Design Engineer responsible for the trenchless portion of the works. This involved the interpretation of geological profiles to generate settlement estimates for multiple potential tunnelling and pipe installation methods. I was also responsible for the recommendation of various tunnelling methodologies including but not limited to, pipe jacking, pipe ramming, and microtunnelling.

The above image demonstrates how the settlement analysis is completed and the effect that the tunnelling process can have on above structures. I completed this calculation for the various installation methodologies, determining which might have the most severe ground impact. This is an important consideration, especially when the tunnelling works aligns beneath major infrastructure. The below image displays the quantification of resulting tunnelling induced ground settlement.

I was also responsible for the corrosion and abrasion design for the horizontal directional drilling (HDD) portion of the works.
