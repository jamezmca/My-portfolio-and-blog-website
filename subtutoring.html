Enthused Tutor

Facilitator of Learning

I have been a mathematics, physics and chemistry tutor since 2013. I have a huge passion for these subjects and find them absolutely fascinating and tutoring provides me with an opportunity to continue my learning in these subjects and also share my enthusiasm for them.

Initially, I was only tutoring subjects I was competent in but have since extended it to any subject I want to learn about. I find I can effectively learn new content and communicate such information in logical and understandable chunks which I find to be a highly effective tutoring style.

Learning together promotes engagement and I try to make every tutorial as fun as possible. If my students haven't laughed in their session then I need to work harder. I strongly believe in the importance of education as it provides so much opportunity to younger generations and I believe sharing my enthusiasm for it helps foster my students' appreciation for learning.
