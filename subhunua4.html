Hunua 4 Watermain

Trenched and Trenchless Pipe Installation

On this project I was a Site Engineer and a client representative where my primary responsibility involved ensuring the construction and installation of the pipeline met the requirements of the design. I provided live technical responses to contractor queries, or 'Requests For Information', when any construction concerns regarding the design arose; problem solving on the go.

This project was especially interesting as we encountered a very unique geological formation - lava caves. The lava caverns resulted in a construction failure on the project which required emergency response. I was lucky to be part of this response and I consequently went on to present about lava caverns, winning a best presentation award from New Zealand Geotechnical Society of Young Engineers.

The emergency response required innovative design solutions utilizing very funky technology - special LIDAR scans, ground penetration radar and consequent image processing software. In a timely manner, my team was required to provide a technical, constructable, and safe design solution to a problem that we had never encountered.
