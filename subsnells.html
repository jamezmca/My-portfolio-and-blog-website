Snells Algies Outfall

Pipeline Ballast System

For this project, I was the design engineer responsible for the structural design of the pipeline outfall. Specifically completing technical design checks and analysis for a variety of elements on the project and completing the design of reinforced ballast blocks required to anchor the marine section of the outfall.

These ballasts were custom designed specifically for the present marine conditions and environment, and a Finite Element Analysis was completed to accurately model the complex structural mechanisms of the system to ensure it anchored the marine outfall to the seabed.
